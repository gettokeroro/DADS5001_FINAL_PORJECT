## English

The **Symptom-to-Specialty Triage** application is an educational project for the DADS5001 course.
**It is not a medical device, not a diagnostic tool, and cannot replace advice from licensed medical professionals.**

The information displayed by this app provides **preliminary suggestions only** regarding:
- Medical specialties potentially relevant to your symptoms
- Urgency levels based on Thailand's MOPH ED Triage standard
- Red-flag symptoms to watch for

This information **cannot replace physical examination by a qualified physician**.
Accurate diagnosis requires history-taking, physical examination, and laboratory tests in combination.

### Seek emergency care immediately if you have:

- Severe chest pain · radiating to left arm/jaw · diaphoresis
- Severe shortness of breath · cyanosis (blue lips/fingertips)
- Facial droop · arm/leg weakness · slurred speech (stroke signs)
- Altered consciousness · seizures · loss of consciousness
- Uncontrolled bleeding · vomiting blood · black stool
- Severe acute abdominal pain

In Thailand: dial **1669** for emergency medical services.

### Limitations

1. Uses an international dataset (itachi9604) covering 41 diseases — **not exhaustive**
2. AI mode may hallucinate or produce incorrect output — always verify
3. Cost estimates are median values, not actual hospital quotes
